Prototype phase

Project: prototyping

" A prototype is a product built to test ideas and changes until it resembles the final product."
Our main goal for this phase is based on what our client what and users needs we find a acceptable design for our website.

This phase of the project is more familiar for our group members because of our previous experience working as a group on designing a website and also in our personal assignments as well.

CMD methods:

Mood board:
I tried to get more information about the Greek ancient culture, the history of the toys, search for motifs that used in old Greek building that might be inspiring to use them in our website, and most colors that are used in ancient toys, architecture, and sculptures.
Literature study:
I tried to get all information above by visiting museums’ websites, artists online portfolio, and some online shops for artworks which are inspired by ancient Greek culture.

Learning outcomes:

• Interactive media
We had the two Figma prototypes and we showed them to our customer and ask for her opinion about each. Also, we got feedback from our front-end development teacher and we found out we have to work more on our prototype.
• Communication:
By having the meeting with our teacher and our client we tried to improve our prototype that helped us in this process to reach the final design.
• Research
Creating the mood board for myself needed research and I got all my information via literature studies for this part.
• Professional identity:
We collect all our information to create a website inspired by artist’s work and Greek culture.
• Design:
Working on both paper and Figma prototype to reach the final design.

Process and results:

First week:

After the card sorting and ideating phase, we started to work on prototyping, at first, we has different sketches and we drew all of them on paper, based on our client and card sorting result we tried to find a good design for the website. We didn’t make the decision in the first week because everyone had a different idea.

Second week:

In the second week, we tried to find the best layout for the page and we finalized our paper prototype. We decided to start working on the Figma prototype based on the paper prototype that we all agreed with. I worked on the contact page.

Figma:

The Figma design should have been something like the final paper prototype but the design of another member for the landing page was not based on what we designed on paper and mostly was based on personal ideas and that layout referred to the other pages too. :(
So the group decided to continue with the new layout because we did not have enough time for changing it and also to convince everyone for the same template. The only thing that we needed to decide about it was the color palette. We arranged a meeting with our client to ask for her opinion on colors.
