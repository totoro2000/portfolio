Principles of design psychology

Empathize phase

The goal of this assignment is to learn about design principles, how to use them, recognize them in different websites, and other forms of media, and how to use them properly in my work and get help from them. The CMD methods that I used for this assignment are:

1. Trend analytics
2. Literature study, I used online resources for this assignment, Which are mentioned in the references part.
3. Comparison chart, for this method I did not use a chart in my research it was just comparing each website with the others and how they include these principles in their design.

One of the challenges that I had during this assignment was the difference between the principle of identity and emotional contagion because, in my opinion, they are kind of related to each other. While during the research the differences became clear.

Questions:

1. What are the principles of design psychology?
2. How trends and habits influenced on deign principles?
3. What is the difference between principle of identity and Principle of emotional contagion?
