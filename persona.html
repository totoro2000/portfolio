Persona

Define phase

For creating the persona for our group project the biggest problem that we had was a lack of information from our client. What’re her wishes for the website, an online shop, or a gallery, or who is exactly the target group of our project children or adults, how can make it playful, and for which target group? So for this assignment, we created two personas based on the first meeting that we had with our client, and also for answering all these questions we arranged an interview with Fenia( client). The next step for this project was thinking more about the persona and also a survey for our target group to make the requirements both for our client and our users clear.

CMD methods:

Observation:
We tried to search what is important for a parents and a retaler to buy a toy/ artwork, what are their most important point. We took note of them and tried to sort them in two group one for a mother and the other for the museum manager.
Ideation
We all know the main subject so as a group we tried to develop our ideas about our user. What are their priority when they want to buy a product. We wrote down all of our information and then created our personas.

Learning outcomes:

• Communication
We got feedback from our mentor which was acceptable and the information that we provided in each persona was complete and also helpful.
• Research
Based on what information we have from our client wishes we created a persona and developed it after our second meeting with her.
